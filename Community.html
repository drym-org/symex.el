10 Community ¶

Where to find and connect with the Symex and broader Emacs community.

  • Symex.el — the Symex project repository. Report bugs, request features, discuss issues, follow updates.
  • #emacs — Symex contributors sometimes hang out on IRC in #emacs. It could be a good place to ask for help.
  • Look for Emacs meetups in your town! Like EmacsSF in the San Francisco Bay Area.
  • ABE Calendar — Symex is part of the ABE community, which meets regularly to work on open source and broader economic and accounting issues.
  • Any other ideas for connecting with fellow Symex users? Let us know!
