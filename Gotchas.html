7.6.7 Gotchas ¶

The symex-traversal form accepts a single traversal argument. If you’d like to do more than one thing, then wrap the steps in a maneuver or a venture.

symex-deftraversal is equivalent to (defvar name (symex-traversal traversal)). As it uses defvar, once defined, you cannot use the same form to redefine the traversal (e.g., if you are debugging it).
