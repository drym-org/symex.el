KeyActionRemarks
h, j, k, lBackwards, down, up, forwards
gj, gkLinewise down, upDon’t rely too heavily on these — e.g. "leap branch" is often better
f, bTraverse forwards, backwards

F, BSkip forwards, backwardsQuick ways to move forwards and backwards — traverse without entering nested expressions
{, }Leap backwards, forwards"Leap" to adjacent branches in the current tree, preserving position on branch
M-{, M-}Soar backwards, forwardsLeap, but crossing trees if necessary
C-k, C-jClimb, descendA quick way to go up and down a tree
