2.6.2 Where Should I Put My Customizations? ¶

For any customizations you had in the :config or :custom sections of the former use-package declaration, you would now typically put those in the corresponding section of the symex package, unless it is specific to an optional package like symex-ide or symex-evil, in which case you should include the config with these packages.
