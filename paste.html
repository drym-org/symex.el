7.2.11 paste ¶

Syntax ¶

(paste [before|after])

or

(drop [before|after])

Description ¶

Paste the “current kill” either before or after the current expression.

Examples ¶

"Emit forward." 

(symex-eval
  (symex-traversal
    (do (move up)
        (repeat (move forward))
        (take this)
        (move down)
        (drop after))))
